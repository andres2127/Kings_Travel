About Us

Kings Travel was founded over 12 years ago with one mission: to help you explore the world with confidence, comfort, and unforgettable experiences. What started as a small travel agency in Barcelona, Spain, has now grown into a global company with a strong presence in more than 8 countries.

Our team is made up of passionate travel experts who specialize in creating unique journeys tailored to every traveler’s dream. We proudly offer curated experiences in top destinations like:

  • Thailand – cultural adventures and tropical getaways
  • Dubai & the Middle East – luxury, innovation, and desert exploration
  • Italy & Western Europe – food tours and romantic escapes
  • Colombia & South America – nature, coffee, and authentic local life

To date, Kings Travel has guided over 50,000 travelers around the globe. We take pride in our personalized service, trusted local partners, and flexible travel plans for every budget and style.

Whether you're planning a honeymoon, backpacking adventure, family vacation, or spiritual retreat — we’re here to make it happen.

Meet Our Team
